The Art

What are the best things to bring for the perfect picnic?

  1. A big blanket, choose a cute colored one if you want it to match a certain aesthetic!
  2. Decide what kind of food you want! Will it be a meal or more of a treat/snack? Fruiit, nuts and cheese make great on a charcuterie baord!
  3. silverware, bring colored silverware to once again match a color scheme
  4. Friends and family are great company for a picnic but also solo picnics can be just as fun and relaxing!

Where should I have my picnic?

  1. A nearby park
  2. Up in the mountains
  3. The beach-a personal fav
  4. A garden
  5. Look for somewhere calm and secluded.
  6. Be creative there are so many options when it comes to a good location for picknciking!
